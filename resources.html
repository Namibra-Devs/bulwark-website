RESOURCES

Welcome to our resource page. We believe that access to reliable information and resources is essential for achieving our mission of improving the health and well-being of women and girls around the West African Sub-Region.On this page, you'll find a curated list of resources related to women's health, including information on sexual and reproductive health. We hope that these resources will be helpful to you and that you'll share them with others who may benefit from this information.

Sexual and Reproductive Health

This category includes information on family planning, contraceptive options, and sexually transmitted infections. It also includes information on pregnancy, childbirth, and menopause. Some of the resources listed in this category include:

  • Planned Parenthood: A trusted source of information on sexual and reproductive health, with resources on birth control, STIs, and pregnancy.
  • Bedsider: A free online birth control support network that offers information, tools, and resources to help women find the right contraceptive method for them.
  • Our Bodies, Ourselves: A nonprofit organization that provides evidence-based information on women's health, including reproductive health, sexuality, and relationships.
